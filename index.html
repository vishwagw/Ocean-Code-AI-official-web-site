The challenge

Real-world data collection
is holding your team back

AI teams everywhere hit the same wall — getting the right training data is slow, expensive, legally risky, and structurally incomplete.

01 —
Months to collect, years to label

Real-world dataset collection and annotation takes months — sometimes years. Your model roadmap can't afford to wait. Competitors who move faster win.

02 —
Prohibitive cost at scale

High-quality, annotated datasets cost £50,000–£500,000+ per project. Enterprise teams overspend on data ops. Startups simply can't compete on even footing.

03 —
GDPR and privacy liability

Real-world footage is saturated with PII, consent obligations, and regulatory exposure. Legal risk alone has stalled or killed more projects than bad model architecture.

04 —
Edge cases remain invisible

The scenarios that matter most — rare events, extreme conditions, safety-critical moments — are the hardest to capture in real data. Your model will fail exactly where it shouldn't.

The platform
